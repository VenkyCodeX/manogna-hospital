About Us

Committed to Your Health & Well-being

Manogna Hospital is a leading healthcare facility located in Abdullahpurmet, Hyderabad. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate, and affordable medical care to every patient who walks through our doors.

Our team of experienced doctors, nurses, and support staff work tirelessly to ensure the highest standards of patient care. Equipped with modern diagnostic tools and treatment facilities, we are prepared to handle everything from routine check-ups to complex medical emergencies.

  • Experienced and qualified medical professionals
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment facilities
  • Patient-first approach in every decision
  • Affordable healthcare for all

Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Services

We offer a wide range of healthcare services to meet all your medical needs under one roof.

General Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses, chronic conditions, and preventive healthcare for all age groups.

Emergency Services

Round-the-clock emergency care with rapid response teams equipped to handle all critical medical situations.

Maternity Care

Complete prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care in a safe and supportive environment for mother and child.

Diagnostics & Lab

Advanced diagnostic imaging, pathology, and laboratory services for accurate and timely results.

Cardiology

Specialized heart care including ECG, echocardiography, and management of cardiovascular conditions.

Orthopedics

Treatment of bone, joint, and muscle disorders including fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries.

Pediatrics

Dedicated healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents with specialized pediatric doctors.

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care services including vision testing, treatment of eye diseases, and minor surgeries.
